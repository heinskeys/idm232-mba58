How To Use Taste Threads

remove How do I search for recipes?

You can search for recipes using the search bar on the homepage. Simply type the recipe name or ingredients, and press enter.

add How do I save my favorite recipes?

To save your favorite recipes, simply click the heart icon on the recipe page. You can view your saved recipes in your profile.

add How do I filter recipes by cuisine?

On the Recipes page, you can filter recipes by selecting the desired cuisine type from the dropdown menu.
